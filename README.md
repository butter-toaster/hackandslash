# hackandslash
The world has fallen into a catastrophic darkness. Fight your way through hordes of monsters, unlock permanent upgrades, reach high scores and achievements. and discover the secrets of this world!

Hey! I'm pleasantly surprised to see you're actually taking the time to read this. This was a ~155 hour project on learning Godot from complete scratch, only as a guy who knew how to draw. With no prior knowledge or expertise, I managed to put together a neat little fighting game.

I acknowledge it's brevity, and it's clunky combat, but take my project with a grain of salt! I'm sure playing it through and humoring me won't be half bad.

The cookies you give me will help fund a 3D printer, so I can print cool decorations for my room. Or maybe some other cool objects they put up in the shop, who knows?

But anyways...

...rate my project fairly, and hopefully positively, as I pray my time and effort won't be in vain. The devlogs detail my journey in learning Godot, and also give a few sneak peeks into the development phases.

Have fun, and hack and slash your way to victory!
